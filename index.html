Our Story

A Decade of
Starlight

For over ten years, ASTRO has been more than just a K-pop group — they have been a consistent source of light for AROHA. Since their debut in February 2016 under Fantagio, MJ, Jinjin, Cha Eun-woo, and Yoon San-ha have navigated the ever-changing tides of the music industry with grace, sincerity, and an unwavering bond that few groups can claim.

From their "refreshing idol" roots with sunny tracks like Hide & Seek to the mature, evocative soundscapes of their later years, ASTRO has never stopped evolving. They have weathered storms, celebrated triumphs, and remained deeply committed to their craft — reminding us all that after the winter, spring will always come.

Today, as they celebrate a full decade together, they stand as a testament to the power of friendship and the enduring impact of music that truly touches the soul.

What Sets Them Apart

The ASTRO Difference

What sets ASTRO apart in a crowded industry isn't just their moves or their visuals — it's their sincerity.

01 —

Emotional Depth

ASTRO is renowned for their ability to deliver songs that resonate on a personal level. Their music balances upbeat feel-good pop with deeply emotional ballads that feel like an open love letter to their fans — from Hide & Seek to Blue Flame, the range is extraordinary.

02 —

Versatility

Masters of duality. One moment they are the bright, youthful group that makes you smile with Candy Sugar Pop; the next, they command the stage with the dramatic intensity of Blue Flame or the haunting beauty of Knock.

03 —

A Family Bond

Their connection as a group is palpable beyond the stage. Genuine, brotherly chemistry that makes fans feel like they are part of something real — a community built on mutual love and quiet, steady support where "AROHA" is more than just a label.
